Development Process


The website shows different aspects of the Nogard Minecraft server. It needed to be easy to navigate and fairly simple with the content.


This website shows all of the different aspects of my Minecraft server, Nogard. It contains many new things that I learned in the process of creating the site, such as having the header/footer hosted on another page, using iframes, having divs side-by-side, and more. This site was also my first attempt at working with APIs, which you can see the results on the "Status". I feel that the website looks very nice and serves its purpose well, but could use a little updating (inclusion of the forum and store pages within the actual site, etc.).
